Muesli

Back to Home

Description

A wholesome blend of rolled oats, nuts, and dried fruits, served with milk or yogurt.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup rolled oats
  • 1/2 cup mixed nuts (almonds, walnuts, etc.)
  • 1/2 cup dried fruits (raisins, cranberries, etc.)
  • 1 tablespoon honey (optional)
  • Milk or yogurt for serving

Steps

  1. In a bowl, combine the rolled oats, mixed nuts, and dried fruits.
  2. Drizzle with honey if desired.
  3. Serve with milk or yogurt on top.
  4. Enjoy as a healthy breakfast or snack.
