Description

A creamy and delicious smoothie made with fresh fruits, yogurt, and a hint of honey.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup fresh fruits (banana, berries, etc.)
  • 1/2 cup yogurt (plain or flavored)
  • 1/2 cup milk (or juice)
  • 1 tablespoon honey (optional)
  • Ice cubes (optional)

Steps

  1. In a blender, combine the fresh fruits, yogurt, milk (or juice), and honey.
  2. Add ice cubes if desired.
  3. Blend until smooth and creamy.
  4. Taste and adjust sweetness if needed.
  5. Pour into glasses and serve immediately.
