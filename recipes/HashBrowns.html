Hash Browns

Back to Home

Description

Delicious crispy hash browns made from grated potatoes, seasoned and fried to perfection.

Ingredients

  • 2 large potatoes
  • 1 small onion (optional)
  • Salt to taste
  • Black pepper to taste
  • Oil for frying

Steps

  1. Peel and grate the potatoes using a box grater or food processor.
  2. If using, finely chop the onion.
  3. In a bowl, combine the grated potatoes, onion, salt, and pepper. Mix well.
  4. Heat oil in a frying pan over medium heat.
  5. Take a handful of the potato mixture and form it into a patty. Place it in the hot oil.
  6. Fry until golden brown on both sides (about 4-5 minutes per side).
  7. Remove from the pan and drain on paper towels to remove excess oil.
  8. Serve hot as a side dish or breakfast item.
