Fruit Salad

Back to Home

Description

A refreshing mix of seasonal fruits, drizzled with honey and served chilled.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups mixed seasonal fruits (e.g., apples, bananas, berries, oranges)
  • 1 tablespoon honey (optional)
  • Juice of 1 lime or lemon
  • Fresh mint leaves for garnish (optional)

Steps

  1. In a large bowl, combine the mixed fruits.
  2. Drizzle with honey and lime (or lemon) juice.
  3. Toss gently to combine.
  4. Garnish with fresh mint leaves if desired.
  5. Serve chilled and enjoy!
