Porridge

Back to Home

Description

A warm and comforting bowl of porridge made with oats, milk, and a touch of honey.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup rolled oats
  • 2 cups milk (or water)
  • 1 tablespoon honey (optional)
  • Fresh fruits or nuts for topping (optional)

Steps

  1. In a saucepan, combine the rolled oats and milk (or water).
  2. Cook over medium heat, stirring occasionally, until the mixture thickens (about 5-7 minutes).
  3. Remove from heat and stir in honey (if using).
  4. Serve warm, topped with fresh fruits or nuts if desired.
